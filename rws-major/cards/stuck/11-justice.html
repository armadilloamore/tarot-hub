Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re trying to make a decision without acknowledging the full truth — your feelings, motives, or past patterns. You may also be holding yourself to impossible standards, overanalyzing, or waiting for complete certainty before choosing a path.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage requires radical honesty with oneself. Justice represents clarity, accountability, and inner alignment. Resistance appears when you avoid a truth that would force a shift in direction, boundaries, or identity.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Overthinking instead of deciding
  • Avoiding accountability for a repeating pattern
  • Trying to make a perfect or consequence-free decision
  • Ignoring emotional truth in favor of logic alone

Coaching Question

“What truth am I avoiding because it would require me to choose differently?”

Practice

  • Write down the decision you're facing and list the real factors influencing you.
  • Identify the most honest choice, not the easiest.
  • Take one action that brings your behavior into alignment with your truth.

Core Insight

“Clarity arrives when you tell yourself the whole truth.”
