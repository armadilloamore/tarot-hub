Internal Block

You may be stuck because you're entangled in a pattern that provides short-term comfort but long-term stagnation. This could be a coping mechanism, attachment, belief, or habit that keeps you feeling safe while quietly limiting your freedom.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage involves confronting shadow desires, fear-based choices, and unconscious bonds. The Devil represents the illusions we cling to — especially the ones built from past wounds. Resistance appears when letting go feels threatening to your identity or sense of safety.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Staying in a familiar but limiting pattern
  • Believing you’re powerless to change
  • Avoiding discomfort, conflict, or independence
  • Numbing instead of feeling or confronting the truth

Coaching Question

“What attachment or habit is giving me comfort but costing me freedom?”

Practice

  • Identify one behavior or belief that keeps you small.
  • Replace it with a tiny alternative choice today.
  • Say aloud: “I am not trapped — the door is already open.”

Core Insight

“Your chains are illusions — you become free the moment you choose differently.”
