Internal Block

Your system is signaling that a chapter is complete. You may be stuck because you're trying to solve problems that belong to a former version of you. The next step requires releasing an outdated role or expectation.

Psychological Frame

This is the individuation pivot point — when your identity must adjust to match your soul's trajectory. Evolution requires pruning. Death symbolizes transformation through letting go, not loss.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Staying loyal to old versions of yourself
  • Avoiding conflict or disappointing others
  • Fear of entering unfamiliar territory
  • Belief that letting go is failure

Coaching Question

“What responsibility or identity have I outgrown?”

Practice

  • Write a closure sentence describing what is ending.
  • Set a date to complete the letting-go action.
  • Recommit to the version of yourself you're becoming.

Core Insight

“Transition is not loss — it’s alignment.”
