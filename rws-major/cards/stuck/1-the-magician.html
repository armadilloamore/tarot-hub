Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re not fully claiming your own agency. Instead of directing your will, you may be scattering it, doubting it, or waiting for external conditions to give you permission. A part of you might be afraid of your own power or potential.

Psychological Frame

This stage of individuation asks you to align intention, clarity, and action. The Magician symbolizes conscious creation — but resistance shows up when internal doubts keep you from believing you can shape your world.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Underestimating your abilities
  • Procrastinating on something that matters
  • Feeling like you need more tools or mastery
  • Letting others’ expectations override your inner direction

Coaching Question

“What power or capability am I pretending I don’t have?”

Practice

  • Set one clear intention in writing.
  • Decide what first action aligns with that intention.
  • Remove one distraction that dilutes your focus.

Core Insight

“Your life shifts when you choose to direct your power instead of waiting for approval.”
