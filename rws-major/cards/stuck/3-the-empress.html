Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re withholding your creativity, comfort, or self-nourishment. A part of you may feel undeserving of abundance or afraid that if you soften, you’ll lose control. You could also be giving too much and receiving too little.

Psychological Frame

This individuation moment asks you to mother yourself — to validate your needs, express your creativity, and allow yourself to be supported. Resistance often comes from conditioning that taught you to minimize or ignore your own needs.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Difficulty receiving support or care
  • Feeling guilty for resting or slowing down
  • Fearing that comfort equals complacency
  • Creative ideas you don’t pursue

Coaching Question

“What nourishment or support am I denying myself?”

Practice

  • Give yourself one act of real comfort or rest.
  • Start a small creative project with no goal except expression.
  • Say aloud: “Receiving strengthens me, not weakens me.”

Core Insight

“Nurturing yourself unlocks your creative flow.”
