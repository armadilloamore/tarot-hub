Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re hesitating at the threshold of a new beginning. Part of you longs to leap, but another part fears appearing naïve, unprepared, or foolish. Your system may be holding you back out of a desire to stay safe within the known.

Psychological Frame

This is the individuation stage where innocence wants to evolve into experience. The Fool represents trust in the emerging path — but your ego may be resisting the vulnerability that comes with starting fresh.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Fear of making the wrong first step
  • Overthinking instead of beginning
  • Worry about judgment from others
  • Waiting for perfect certainty or timing

Coaching Question

“What step have I been avoiding because I’m afraid to begin imperfectly?”

Practice

  • Choose one small beginner’s action and do it today.
  • Reduce the goal to its simplest, most playful version.
  • Say aloud: “I’m allowed to start before I feel ready.”

Core Insight

“Beginnings require innocence — not expertise.”
