Internal Block

You may feel stuck because you’re moving in extremes — all or nothing, too fast or too slow, overworking then collapsing, giving too much then withdrawing completely. Your system may be overwhelmed, trying to find a middle path but not knowing how to stabilize.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage teaches integration. Temperance represents harmony between opposites — instinct and reason, rest and action, vulnerability and strength. Resistance appears when inner imbalance feels familiar or when moderation feels “not enough.”

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Overcorrecting instead of adjusting
  • Seeing choices in black-or-white terms
  • Trying to fix everything at once
  • Neglecting emotional regulation

Coaching Question

“What would a balanced, middle-way step look like here?”

Practice

  • Identify the two extremes pulling you.
  • Name the midpoint between them.
  • Choose one action that represents gentle moderation.

Core Insight

“Small, steady adjustments create real transformation.”
