Internal Block

You may feel stuck because you’re disconnected from your intuition or ignoring inner signals that contradict what others expect from you. Your system may be overwhelmed by noise — information, opinions, external pressures — making it difficult to trust your inner knowing.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage calls you inward. The High Priestess represents the unconscious guiding you toward deeper truth. Resistance surfaces when stillness feels uncomfortable, or when intuitive answers conflict with what you wish were true.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Avoiding quiet because answers might require change
  • Doubting your inner voice
  • Wanting logical certainty over intuitive truth
  • Overconsuming information instead of listening inward

Coaching Question

“What inner truth have I been avoiding because it asks something of me?”

Practice

  • Sit in silence for 2–3 minutes and notice one intuitive whisper.
  • Journal the first truth that arises without editing.
  • Reduce external input for 24 hours to reconnect with your inner voice.

Core Insight

“Your inner guidance is already speaking — the key is making space to hear it.”
