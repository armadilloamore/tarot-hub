Internal Block

You may feel stuck because your willpower is divided — one part of you wants to move forward while another part hesitates or pulls away. This internal friction creates the sense that no matter how hard you try, momentum keeps slipping.

Psychological Frame

This stage of individuation requires internal coherence. The Chariot symbolizes unified direction — ego and instinct moving together. Resistance arises when fear, doubt, or conflicting desires scatter your energy.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Feeling pulled in two directions
  • Lack of clarity about your destination
  • Starting strong, then losing momentum
  • Avoiding emotional work underlying your goals

Coaching Question

“What part of me is pulling in the opposite direction — and why?”

Practice

  • Name your primary goal for the next 30 days.
  • Identify one behavior that supports this goal and one that undermines it.
  • Commit to one supportive behavior daily.

Core Insight

“Momentum comes from aligning all parts of yourself with the same direction.”
