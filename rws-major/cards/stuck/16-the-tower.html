Internal Block

You may feel stuck because you’re trying to hold together something that is already collapsing — an identity, belief, relationship, or structure that no longer supports your growth. A part of you may fear the fallout of letting the truth break through, so you cling harder even as the cracks widen.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage involves the dismantling of false frameworks — the ego’s earthquake that reveals what is real. The Tower represents necessary disruption. Resistance appears when you fight the breakdown instead of allowing the breakthrough.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Denying what’s clearly changing
  • Trying to fix something meant to end
  • Fearing emotional upheaval
  • Clinging to stability at any cost

Coaching Question

“What truth have I been avoiding because it would require everything to change?”

Practice

  • Acknowledge one structure in your life that feels unstable.
  • Name the truth you’ve been afraid to admit.
  • Let one piece that’s already falling go — consciously.

Core Insight

“When false foundations crumble, your true path is revealed.”
