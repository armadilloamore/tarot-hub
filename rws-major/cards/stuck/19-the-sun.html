Internal Block

You may feel stuck because you’re dimming your light — downplaying your joy, clarity, or authentic self-expression. Part of you may fear being “too much,” too visible, too successful, or too different from those around you. A deeper part may even believe joy is unsafe or temporary.

Psychological Frame

This individuation moment asks you to claim your vitality and celebrate your essence. The Sun represents confidence and inner radiance. Resistance shows up when old conditioning taught you to shrink, mask, or limit your brightness.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Minimizing your achievements or gifts
  • Fear of attention or visibility
  • Distrusting happiness or waiting for it to disappear
  • Feeling guilty for wanting a joyful life

Coaching Question

“What part of me is afraid to shine without apology?”

Practice

  • Name one talent or quality you’ve been downplaying.
  • Let yourself enjoy something without self-judgment.
  • Say aloud: “Joy is safe for me. My light is welcome here.”

Core Insight

“When you shine, your path becomes clear.”
