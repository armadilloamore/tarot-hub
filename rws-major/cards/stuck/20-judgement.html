Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re resisting a calling or transformation that has been building for a long time. Part of you may fear leaving behind an old identity or stepping into a more expansive version of yourself. You might also be held back by guilt, self-judgment, or unresolved past narratives.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage is about awakening — answering the call of your deeper self. Judgement represents integration, truth-telling, and rebirth. Resistance occurs when the past feels unfinished or when becoming your true self threatens an outdated role.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Feeling unworthy of a new chapter
  • Holding onto old stories or shame
  • Ignoring a calling that keeps resurfacing
  • Fear of what others will think if you transform

Coaching Question

“What part of me is calling to rise — and what am I afraid it will cost?”

Practice

  • Write a forgiveness statement to your past self.
  • Identify one truth you’re finally ready to admit.
  • Take a small step that aligns with your higher calling.

Core Insight

“You’re being asked to answer your own awakening.”
