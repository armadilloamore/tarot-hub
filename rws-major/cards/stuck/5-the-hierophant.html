Internal Block

You may be stuck because you’re following rules, traditions, beliefs, or expectations that no longer match who you’re becoming. A part of you may fear breaking from the familiar path or disappointing the authority figures internalized in your psyche.

Psychological Frame

This individuation stage centers on questioning inherited truths. The Hierophant symbolizes meaning-making — but when blocked, he represents outdated dogma or unconscious loyalty to systems you’ve outgrown.

Where You May Be Resisting

  • Feeling guilty for wanting something different
  • Allowing old beliefs to dictate present choices
  • Fear of being unconventional or rebellious
  • Confusing safety with conformity

Coaching Question

“What belief or rule am I following that no longer supports my growth?”

Practice

  • Name one inherited belief that feels restrictive.
  • Rewrite it into a personal truth that empowers you.
  • Do one small action today that aligns with your authentic values.

Core Insight

“Your truth evolves — and you’re allowed to follow it.”
