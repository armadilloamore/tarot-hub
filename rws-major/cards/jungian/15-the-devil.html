The Devil

Jungian Archetypes & The Shadow

Back to Jungian Draw
ArchetypeThe Shadow, The Tempter, The Repressed Self, The Addict, The Saboteur
Psychological RoleRepresents the confrontation with the unconscious shadow, repressed desires, and internal bondage
Ego StatusThe ego is enslaved by unconscious drives—fear, shame, addiction, or denial
Symbolic Number15 (1 + 5 = 6) — a distorted mirror of The Lovers; union corrupted by domination or illusion
Shadow AspectEnslavement, self-deception, projection, fear of freedom, compulsions, and spiritual blindness
Spiritual MeaningOpportunity for radical liberation through confronting one’s inner darkness and false identities
Unconscious ConnectionThe Devil is the raw power of the shadow—what we reject or fear in ourselves, now seeking integration
Journey ThemeThe seeker must reclaim power by facing what they avoid—including shame, lust, fear, or control
Jungian GoalConscious integration of the shadow—turning chains of repression into tools for liberation and wholeness
