ArchetypeThe Tamer of the Beast, Inner Strength, The Integration of Instinct, Compassionate Warrior
Psychological RoleRepresents the conscious ego learning to befriend and integrate the instinctual or shadow self
Ego StatusThe ego moves from external control (The Chariot) to inner mastery through gentleness and courage
Symbolic Number8 — power, regeneration, rhythm, karma, the eternal loop (∞ symbol often shown)
Shadow AspectRepression of instinct, passive aggression, weakness disguised as virtue, or brute force
Spiritual MeaningTrue power comes from inner alignment, patience, and loving control—not domination
Unconscious ConnectionThe lion symbolizes raw instinct or libido—not suppressed, but lovingly guided and honored
Journey ThemeLearning to trust in quiet power, integrating the shadow with heart-centered awareness
Jungian GoalIndividuation through integration—ego and instinct working in harmony, not opposition
